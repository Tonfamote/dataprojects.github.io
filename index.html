DIGITALPATH INNOVATIONS USER EXPERIENCE ANALYSIS USING TABLEAU

DigitalPath Innovations, a leader in digital marketing solutions, embarked on a mission to elevate their website's user experience to improve engagement metrics, conversion rates, and customer satisfaction. The company conducted an A/B test over a two-week period, presenting two distinct versions of its webpage (Version A and Version B) to a randomized group of users and as a key Analyst, I led the data analysis for the results obtained. The aim of the experiment was to identify which webpage version performed better across various user engagement metrics. Also, conversion rate optimization, user feedback analysis, content interaction and referral source impact were thoroughly analysis in this project. This comparative analysis between the two different webpages revealed distinct strengths and weaknesses. By addressing these insights and recommendations, the Company was able to create a more effective, user-centric design that drives engagement, conversion, and business growth.
